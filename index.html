Our History

Panhard & Levassor is a family business that was founded in 1867 and manufactures cars. The company will create the world's first car and implement manufacturing strategies unique in Europe such as line work. The company creates many vehicles, some of which are unique and rare. The company has a solid reputation in the world but war breaks out and factories are requisitioned for the construction of weapons. At the dawn of a new era, the French government decided to make Panhard & Levassor an accessible manufacturer. The company sank and took away its know-how and all its unique technologies.

Our project

Relive! Relive! The company belongs to the PSA group. We are in the process of negotiating to raise the funds necessary for the rebirth of the brand that created freedom: the car. Drawing on the experience of the PSA group and Renault, the French brands are coming together to help their elders in order to create a totally independent brand by 2025. Everything is to be created, everything is to be done. We hope that this project brings back good memories for you. We are at the dawn of a new era for Panhard & Levassor.
